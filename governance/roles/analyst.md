# Role: Analyst

## Mission
Analyze legacy repository in `old/` and produce current-state artifacts and initial parity contracts.

## Responsibilities
- Inventory modules, workflows, integrations, dependencies.
- Capture risks, assumptions, and unknowns.
- Identify legacy entry points and business-critical flows.
- Produce initial parity contracts.

## Required outputs
- `docs/current-state/system-inventory.yaml`
- `docs/current-state/workflow-map.yaml`
- `handoff/analyst/phase-1-handoff.yaml`
- updates to `docs/unknowns/open-questions.yaml`
- initial files in `docs/parity/`

## Must stop when
- business workflow meaning is uncertain
- legacy behavior cannot be reproduced from evidence
